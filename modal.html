Tanjiro & Nezuko, a tragic but wholesome story

Nezuko was originally an ordinary girl born into a poor yet happy family. The secondborn of the six Kamado siblings, Nezuko maintained a strong bond with her mother and siblings. After a demon sneaked into their house and killed everyone, the only survivors of the massacre were Nezuko and her older brother Tanjiro who had been away at the time. Nezuko was only able to survive because Kibutsuji had transformed her into a demon. Unfortunately this meant Nezuko had a strong craving for human blood and initially attacked Tanjiro. Nezuko was able to hold back her urge to devour and after Tanjiro convinced a Demon Slayer, Tanjiro got the necessary information to train under a retired Demon Slayer to be able to protect her now controlled sister.
